# Bank Customer Churn Analysis
## Shifting from Reactive Customer Service to Proactive Data-Driven Retention

## Project Overview
In the competitive banking market, customer retention is the ultimate driver 
of sustainable growth. This project analyzes bank customer data to identify 
the key factors driving customer exits and provide actionable insights that 
allow banks to intervene early and reduce avoidable revenue loss.

## Dataset
[Bank Customer Churn (Maven Analytics)](https://mavenanalytics.io)
- 10,000 rows × 13 columns
- Features: Geography, Age, Balance, Credit Score, Activity Status, Credit Card

## Key Findings
1. **France dominates the customer base** (~50%) but has a proportionally 
   lower exit rate. Germany exits at a disproportionately higher rate — 
   a major retention risk
2. **Overall exit rate is ~20%** — 1 in 5 bank customers is leaving
3. **Inactive members exit at nearly double the rate** of active members — 
   engagement is a strong predictor of retention
4. **Age is a significant factor** — exited customers have a median age of 
   ~45 vs ~36 for retained customers
5. **Balance and credit card ownership show minimal impact** on exit decisions
